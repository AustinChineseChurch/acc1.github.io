First Time Visit

Dear friends, welcome to Austin Chinese Church. If you're exploring a path of faith, we welcome you to join us on this journey. If you're seeking a church home, we invite you to consider Austin Chinese Church as your spiritual family. If you desire to be a disciple of Jesus, we welcome you to walk with us on the lifelong journey of following Him. If you're eager to use your gifts, we invite you to serve alongside us with a humble servant's heart. Welcome, and may you find warmth, fellowship, and spiritual growth here.

  • Visit the Church

    We currently hold services at the main campus and the Southwest Gathering Point. On Sundays we have English, Cantonese and Madarin services. Sunday schools for adults, youth, and childcare are also provided.
