Sunday Worship Schedule

We hold three worship services every Sunday in the main campus, offering services in English, Mandarin, and Cantonese. Please find the detailed worship schedule below. The Southwest Campus is at service as well. In addition, we offer adult Sunday school, youth Sunday school, and childcare services. Regardless of language or age, we have worship services tailored for your whole family. Join us in praising and worshiping God together! The church also provides lunch services, and we offer free lunch for first-time visitors. The Southwest Campus service time: 10:00 AM - 12:35 PM (For more details, please see check Website ):
