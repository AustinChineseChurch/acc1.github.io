Established in 1982, Austin Chinese Church remains unaffiliated with any denomination, embracing devout Christians from diverse backgrounds. United in purpose, we faithfully follow the Lord's teachings and spread the gospel while steadfastly building the church. Over decades, we've been blessed with continuous growth, resulting in two branches.
We currently hold services at the main campus and the Southwest Gathering Point. On Sundays at the main campus, English worship and Mandarin/Cantonese Sunday School begin at 9:30 AM, followed by Mandarin worship, Cantonese worship, and English Sunday School at 11:10 AM. At the Southwest Gathering Point, Mandarin and English Sunday School starts at 10:00 AM, followed by Mandarin worship and English worship at 11:15 AM.
Both locations offer childcare, and children and teenagers are organized by age for Gospel-based learning. We eagerly anticipate your involvement and wish for God's blessings to be with you and your family always.
